**Key finding from research:** In Next.js App Router, each feature route needs its own `error.tsx` because:
1. Route-level errors preserve the layout (sidebar stays visible; only page body shows error)
2. Recovery actions can be context-appropriate per route
3. Loading skeletons match actual page structure, reducing CLS
